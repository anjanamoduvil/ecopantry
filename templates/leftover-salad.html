Leftover Salad Recipe

A creative and nutritious way to turn leftovers into a delicious salad.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup leftover cooked vegetables
  • 1/2 cup cooked rice, pasta, or grains
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh herbs (mint, parsley, or coriander)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp lemon juice or vinegar
  • 1 tsp mustard or yogurt (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all your leftover cooked veggies and grains.
  2. Chop them into bite-sized pieces if needed.
  3. In a mixing bowl, add vegetables, grains, and herbs.
  4. Whisk olive oil, lemon juice, mustard/yogurt, salt, and pepper into a dressing.
  5. Pour the dressing over the mixture and toss gently to coat.
  6. Let it sit in the fridge for 10–15 minutes for flavors to blend.
  7. Serve chilled with a sprinkle of extra herbs or seeds on top!
