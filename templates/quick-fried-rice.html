Quick Fried Rice

Transform your leftover rice into a tasty, quick dish!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cold cooked rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, etc.)
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients and chop vegetables finely.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add garlic and onion, sauté until fragrant.
  4. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 4–5 minutes.
  5. Scramble an egg if using, then mix with the veggies.
  6. Add rice, break up clumps, and stir well.
  7. Add soy sauce, seasonings, and mix evenly.
  8. Fry for a couple more minutes and serve hot.
