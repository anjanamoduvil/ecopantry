Banana Bread Recipe

A perfect way to turn ripe bananas into a sweet and satisfying treat.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1.5 cups all-purpose flour
  • Optional: chocolate chips, chopped nuts

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 175°C (350°F). Grease a loaf pan or line with parchment paper.
  2. Peel and mash the bananas in a large bowl until smooth.
  3. Stir in the melted butter until combined.
  4. Add sugar, egg, and vanilla; mix well.
  5. Sprinkle the baking soda and salt over the mixture and mix in.
  6. Add flour last and stir until just combined. Fold in optional ingredients if using.
  7. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 50-60 minutes.
